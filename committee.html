What is the committee


The preschool committee is the body responsible for running the charity and has oversight over the day to day running of the setting which is managed by the setting manager. The Committee consists of the 3 main roles Chair, Treasurer and Secretary plus a number of other individuals as deemed neccessary.


We are currently recruiting for committee members all neccessary training is provided and we would love to hear from anyone who may be interested in helping out. If you may be interested please feel free to contact any member of the committee or speak to the setting manager
